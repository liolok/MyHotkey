[h3]Some hotkeys which may help you as well.[/h3]

No need to restart game, [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3445951083]customize shortcuts[/url] in bottom of Settings > Controls page.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[h3]General[/h3]
[list]
[*]Drop Lantern (or Light Bulb if no Lantern)
[*]Open/Close Polar Bearger Bin
[*]Walking Cane: Equip a speed-up hand tool, or switch back to former hand equipment.
(Priority: Walking Cane > The Lazy Explorer > Wooden Walking Stick > Thulecite Club > Speedy Balloon)
[*]Jump In / Travel via: Wormhole, Big Slimy Pit, Time Rift, Sinkhole, Stairs
[*]Open/Close Umbralla: If no Umbralla on ground, will try to drop one and open.
[*]Murder (Will ignore Scorching Sunfish & Ice Bream)
[*]Save Game (press twice or hold to confirm action)
[*]Reset Game: Restart the server from the last save. (press twice or hold to confirm action)
[*]Toggle Movement Prediction
[/list]
